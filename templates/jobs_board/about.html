⚡ Why I Built JobPulse

When I moved to the UK and started applying for jobs, I hit a wall — pure silence. Hundreds of applications, no responses. I had no idea what the market actually wanted, which skills were in demand, or whether my CV was even getting past ATS filters.

"Instead of applying blindly, I built a platform to understand the market. JobPulse now automatically fetches 500+ UK job postings daily, analyses them with Claude AI, and tells me exactly what skills are missing from my CV."

JobPulse is the project I built while job hunting — and it's the project that taught me Docker, FastAPI, Celery, Redis, PostgreSQL, GitHub Actions and AWS deployment. It's not just a portfolio piece. I use it every day.
